Before Whom He Stands

A voice. A mantle.
A fire that never dies.

Pastor Olumide Oni carries a prophetic mandate for this generation — to raise a people who stand before the Lord, know His voice, and walk in the fullness of His Spirit.

Alongside his wife, Pastor (Mrs.) Oluwatoyin Oni, he ministers the unchanging Word of God with conviction, compassion, and power — seeing lives transformed through the eternal truth of the Gospel.
