The Calling

Four mantles. One anointing.

Prophet.
Evangelist.
Pastor.
Teacher.

Pastor Olumide Oni moves in the fullness of the fivefold ministry — a prophet who hears and declares, an evangelist whose message wins souls, a pastor who shepherds with compassion, and a teacher whose word dispels doubt and builds faith. Above all, he is a husband and father — a man who orders his private life before God first, and whose public ministry flows from that altar.

Together in Ministry Covenant, calling, and the work of the Kingdom
A Shared Ministry

Two standing together before the Lord.

Pastor Olumide Oni and his wife, Pastor (Mrs.) Oluwatoyin Oni, have labored side by side in the vineyard of the Lord for many years — blessed with children, steadfast in covenant, and united in purpose.

Wherever the Master has sent them, they have gone. Through every reassignment, every new field of ministry, and every season of trial, they have stood together as co-laborers — a living testimony that a house built on prayer, faith, and the unchanging Word of God cannot be shaken.

Pastoral Journey

A path of obedience and appointment.

From region to region within the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries — every assignment a new altar, every relocation a fresh commissioning.

  1. The Beginning

    MFM Kubwa

    The starting point of a long obedience — where the call was first formally set in motion.

  2. First Reassignment

    MFM Lugbe Region, Abuja

    Transferred to serve in the Lugbe Region of the Federal Capital Territory, Nigeria.

  3. Capital Service

    MFM Utako → Wuye, Abuja

    Reassigned to MFM Utako, which later transitioned to MFM Wuye — designated as the International Headquarters Annex in Abuja, Nigeria.

  4. Present Assignment

    MFM North America HQ · Prayer City

    Transferred from MFM Wuye to the MFM North America Headquarters — known as Prayer City — where he currently serves as Principal Regional Overseer.

Ministry of the Word

A voice that dispels doubt and demonstrates power.

Pastor Olumide Oni is a dynamic minister of God's Word. His teachings carry a remarkable ability to silence doubt while unveiling the extraordinary power of God — through prayers that produce tangible results, astonishing manifestations, and awe-inspiring miracles.

His personal encounters through life's trials stand as a living testament to the unshakable strength of God to deliver His people in every season of adversity. He ministers not from theory, but from the altar where he himself has stood and seen the hand of the Lord.
